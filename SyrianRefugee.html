DO YOU KNOW ABOUT THE SYRIAN REFUGEE CRISIS?

The Syrian Refugee Crisis is labelled as one of the largest refugee and displacement crisis in the world. But most of the people do not know the scale of the crisis, how long it spans, what caused it and how many refugees have fled crisis in Syria. Even now the crisis, is still escalating and producing more refugees in need of monetary support and aid from other countries.

GOAL

Our main goal with this project is to bring awareness to the people about the crisis and motivate them to help out as much as they can.

DESIGN

We designed our project as a narrative scrollytelling visualization to show the story of the Syrian Refugee crisis with data extracted from unhcr(UN Refugee Agency). As we scroll the visualization shows the number of refugees on the y axis, and the top 15 asylum countries on the x-axis.Each pixel in our visualization represents 10,000 people. The sparkline on the right shows the number of refugees every year. We represent the increasing number of refugees every year by intensifying the colors of the pixels.

REFLECTION

Working on this project really taught me how well data visualization can reinforce the story and communicate a strong message that cannot be done with general storytelling. I learnt how to design a visualization with the available data so that most of the important information that is spread across different sources can be compiled into a single place. I am very happy with the way the first iteration of the project turned out, though improvements like adding a geographical element could make it better. Overall working with my team, not only taught me technical skills and teamwork, but also the important role that technology can play in educating people about important social and humanitarian issues.
